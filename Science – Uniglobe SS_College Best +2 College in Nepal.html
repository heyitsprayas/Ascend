+2 Science Program

Your Gateway to
a Successful Future

Explore by Learning

Uniglobe Secondary School +2 Science program is a dynamic hub for aspiring business leaders. Fueled by a passion for understanding how organisation works, students embark on a comprehensive journey that equips them with the skills to succeed in today’s ever-changing marketplace. Led by experienced instructors with real-world experience, the program goes beyond textbooks. Students get to put their learning into action through interactive exercises, case studies, and simulations, making the knowledge truly their own.

Uniglobe Secondary School modern classrooms provide a stimulating environment for learning. Here, students develop their critical thinking and problem-solving skills through engaging discussions and group projects. They learn the importance of effective communication, teamwork, and strategic decision-making – all essential ingredients for a successful business career. As students delve into finance, marketing, and leadership principles, they’re well on their way to becoming the next generation of innovative and impactful business leaders.

GRADE XII

COMPULSORY SUBJECTS-II

  • Comp. English
  • Comp. Nepali
  • Comp Mathematics
  • Chemistry
  • Physics

Optional Subject – I (any one)

  • Biology
  • Computer Science

Eligibility For +2 Science

To start on this exciting journey in business, we seek students with a strong foundation in core subjects. While a minimum SEE GPA of 1.6 is important, we also value a well-rounded understanding of communication and analytical skills. Therefore, a minimum D+ in English, Nepali, and Mathematics is desired. Additionally, exposure to Social Studies and Science provides valuable context for navigating the complexities of the business world.

Frequently ask Questions

Unsure about something? Don’t worry! We are here to answer all your thoughts about Uniglobe Secondary school
What are the types of programs offered by Uniglobe Secondary School?
Uniglobe Secondary School offers a diverse range of programs to equip you for your future career. We currently specialise in three distinct fields: Science, Management, and Law. Each program provides a strong foundation in its respective area, with Science focusing on scientific exploration and practical skills, Management fostering business acumen and leadership, and Law delving into legal principles and their practical applications.
What is the admission process for Uniglobe Secondary School?

Uniglobe Secondary School helps to make the admission process smooth and straightforward. Here’s a breakdown of the steps involved:

  1. Get Counselling: Schedule a personalised counselling session to discuss program options.
  2. Fill Admission Form: Download the application form from our website or pick one up from admissions.
  3. Practice Paper: Prepare for the entrance exam with our practice papers and sample questions.
  4. Apply Now: Submit your completed application and supporting documents before the deadline.
What are the benefits of attending Uniglobe Secondary School?
Uniglobe Secondary School offers a student-centric learning environment with experienced faculty, modern facilities, and a focus on practical skills development. We also emphasise personalised guidance and support to ensure your academic success.
What kind of facilities does Uniglobe Secondary School offer?
Uniglobe Secondary School provides a dynamic learning environment with modern classrooms, well-equipped labs for hands-on learning, a vast library for research, and tech-enabled teaching. Beyond academics, the college offers sports facilities and comfortable study spaces for a well-rounded student experience.

Uniglobe Secondary School in Kathmandu, Nepal, offers +2 programs in Science, Management and Law.
