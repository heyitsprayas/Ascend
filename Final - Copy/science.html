About the Science Program

The +2 Science program at Uniglobe is designed for future doctors, engineers, and scientists. With modern labs, experienced instructors, and practical-based learning, students gain a strong foundation in Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics.

Courses Offered

Physics

Strong conceptual and experimental foundation for aspiring engineers and researchers.

Chemistry

Modern labs and hands-on experiments for future scientists and doctors.

Biology

Comprehensive program for students aiming for careers in medicine and life sciences.

Eligibility for +2 Science

Admission Criteria

Minimum SEE GPA of 2.0 with D+ in English, Mathematics, and Science. Students must have a strong interest in pursuing careers in STEM fields.
